How the $NPC Ecosystem Functions

The $NPC cycle is a beautiful, self-sustaining loop of human behavior:

  1. Detection: Our proprietary bots detect a shift in social media sentiment (e.g., "#NPC" mentions increase by 0.5%).
  2. Signal Amplification: Key influencers (holders of >1B $NPC) receive a neural impulse to post memes.
  3. Consensus Formation: The community sees the memes, experiences a shared emotional response (FOMO), and a buy consensus is reached subconsciously.
  4. Action: Wallets connected to the $NPC network execute a "Reflexive Buy Order" (RBO). The chart turns green.
  5. Validation: The green chart is posted back to social media, proving the initial sentiment was correct. The loop repeats.

It's not trading. It's synchronized market respiration.
